Financial Planning is the basis for wealth creation. The process of wealth creation will fetch the desired outcome if personal finances are planned well.

## Why plan your finances?

A person’s ability to save and invest depends on so many nuances like his/her spending habits, risk appetite, disposable income and the urge/mindset to save. A plan which will help the client and us understand the above nuances will go a long way in the process of wealth creation by quantifying the risk-reward relationship, devise the right asset-allocation, thereby helping the client achieve the desired outcome.
