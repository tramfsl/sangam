Financial Planning is the basis for wealth creation. The process of wealth creation will not fetch the desired outcome if personal finances are not planned well. 

## Why plan your finances? 
A person’s ability to save and invest depends on so many factors like his spending habits, risk appetite, disposable income and the urge/mindset to save. A plan which will help the client as well as understand the nuances will go a long way in the process of wealth creation by quantifying the risk-reward relationship, devise the right asset-allocation, thereby helping the client achieve the desired outcome.
